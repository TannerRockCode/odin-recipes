Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

This recipe guides you to create a chicken alfredo pasta that will have friends and family raving. In this recipe you will be guided on prepping the chicken to ensure the meat is soft and tender, and that the sauce is balanced and full of flavor. This recipe includes making the alfredo sauce which is key to creating this gormet quality entree. While we do recommend following the full instructions to create the homestyle alfredo sauce, if time does not permit feel free to substitute for canned alfredo as needed.

Ingredients

  • 3 Bonelss chicken breasts (2 lbs.)
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 cup Shredded mozzerela
  • 1 cup Grated parmesan
  • 1 Tbsp Chopped parsely
  • 10 oz. Goat cheese
  • 10 oz. Fettucine

Instructions

  1. Let chicken breast rest in iced milk for 15 minutes to soften meat
  2. Heat large skillet to 400 fehrenheit. Add olive oil to coat pan to prevent meat from sticking to pan surface. Cook chicken for 8 minutes eat side and until chicken has cooked through to 375 fehrenheit.
  3. Add butter to pan and gently sauce the chicken.
  4. Add heavy cream and fold on chicken continously until combined.
  5. Bring pot to boil to cook pasta in. Upon boil, add the pasta to the water and cook 8 minutes until noodles have softened.
  6. In the pan with chicken cream, add the mozzeralla, parmesian, and fetuccine while the pan simmers. Continue to stir to incorporate the melting cheese.
  7. Once noodles have cooked, drain the water from the noodle pot using a filter.
  8. Toss pasta, sauce, and chicken. Garnish with parsely.
